May 18th - May 24th Learning Review

This past week while I was productive, I did get caught up in a particular activity that completely slowed my progress. I am not completely satisfied with my task accomplishments and am now behind schedule but I may have a slight opportunity to catch up. It’ll be a bit tough but not impossible.

My intended focuses:

  • Complete OverTheWire Bandit CTF.
  • Update Passwords
  • Start Unreal Engine Learning
  • Finish Up Final Work for the Cyber Range

Repeating Activities include:

  • Continue Web Developer Bootcamp
  • Read and complete PFsense Book/Course
  • HTB (Retired) Walkthroughs
  • Apply for Jobs

Additional Non-Focused Activities:

  • Improve OpenVPN Autodeployment script (v1.2)
  • Hackintosh VM
  • Security Onion Research
  • Pivot Tables Research

Review

No I did not finish a lot, and there is only about to be more added on for next week. What I did finish was the final work for the Cyber Range. While I did work on Web Development Bootcamp it was pretty minimal. I did some HTB walkthroughs and did Apply for just a few jobs, though I could improve the amount I applied to. Finally, I did much, much more on the Hackintosh VM project that I expected.

I went quite in-depth with the Hackintosh. Not only did I attempt to build a VM, but I deployed two bare-metal instances and did an absolute ton of research on how to get them fully working. Unfortunately I did not deploy a perfectly working version, but I was able to get it up and running and usable for research. There is still more work to do on it but after three full days of research I need to move on. It was a fun project to learn about, but today I am suffering a few consequences of my experimentation.

With the pfSense course I did not finish. I got distracted, but I am officially setup and installed now so things should move quickly. I’m upset that I did get so distracted and ignored my responsibilities for the week. The same thing applies to the CTF, Passwords, and Unreal Engine Learning.

Takeaway

I’m not proud of this week. I’ll be in debt for the upcoming week substantially and it is all me being lazy. To improve, I am going to have to make my schedules and follow them more accurately. My problem last week is I did not respect the time constraints on my projects.

Thanks for listening, and see you tomorrow for an deceleration.
