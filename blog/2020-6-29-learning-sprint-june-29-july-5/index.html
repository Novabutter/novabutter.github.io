June 29-July 5 Learning

Last week was a massive failure. While I was busy with things I found worthwhile, I did not follow the tasks at hand. If you take a look at the previous week it appears I finished nothing but behind the scenes I had a lot going on that I still cannot yet mention. What I can mention is this: I had a ton of fun with the Windows Exploit Development Post and it was a great review since I had taken the course. Really inspires me in some ways and definitely check it out. Back to this week I have been able to slowly chop off my intended focuses, but my time for pure productivity is running out so the game is afoot in finishing my rollover so I can actually progress in my learning instead of standing in idle water. Here is what I plan on attacking this week.

My intended focuses:

As for the rest of the items, they need to be done anyway and now that I have some time I feel like I can accomplish them efficiently again. I’ll get all of these items checked off and all you have to do is sit back and watch.
