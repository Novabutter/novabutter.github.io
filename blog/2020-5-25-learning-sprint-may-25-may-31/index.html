May 25 - May 31 Learning

For the previous week I get extremely side-tracked with a few specific projects. As they were not related to my progress in shifting my actual progress and more relating to personal interest. And that issue cannot seep into this week. Thankfully that is simply not a luxury I can afford anymore; too much more of a slip and I’ll be significantly off schedule. While schedule isn’t everything and I am human, I truly am going to try to stick to it. Here is my self-inflected tough schedule.

My intended focuses:

  • Complete OverTheWire Bandit CTF.
  • Update Passwords
  • Start Unreal Engine Learning
  • Windows Exploit Development Course Review Post

Repeating Activities include:

  • Finish Web Developer Bootcamp
  • Read and complete PFsense Book/Course
  • Study for PCNSA Exam
  • Start Active Directory Learning
  • HTB (Retired) Walkthroughs
  • Apply for Jobs

Additional Non-Focused Activities:

  • Improve OpenVPN Autodeployment script (v1.2)
  • Security Onion Research
  • Pivot Tables Research

This is not only a lot due to the rollover from last week, but most of the repeating activities are all a heavy workload. Web Dev Bootcamp has at least half of the course left, the PFsense course still not half done, starting to study Active Directory, and definitely apply for more jobs than last week. It is going to be an absolute ton of work.

Now I did sign up for the Palo Alto Certified Network Security Administrator. My test date is June 25th, and I start studying this week. I also have a few other matters to attend to this week that’ll keep me busy.

I most likely DEFINITELY will not complete everything on this list. Atop everything else I still won’t have a ton of time as I am moving out of my apartment and entertaining guests this week (my family). It will be a crunch for time to complete these items but at the same time a relief to see my family. I hope to just get a few things done to be honest. Don’t forget to checkout my Trello Board to see what I am working on day-to-day.
